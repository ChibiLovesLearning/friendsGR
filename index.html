I had a few things to say and I thought that instead of my amazing words of mass ego destruction, why not write a virtual letter? I know these are rough days for all of us. To the point that we can't deny it anymore. But we gotta do what we can do, the best of ourselves and sometimes for each other. It is daunting to open up and who would know it better than both of us? But I still want to tell you that since these are trying times, emotionally, mentally and physically, with so many change going on or us anticipating the change that is delayed, we have to look out for each other because being friends is all about being together. Trippy said one thing to me some days ago. Families won't stay, if you really think about it. But you will grow older with your friends. Just saying that it doesn't have to a one way street. I understand if you don't want to be vulnerable but I do want to assure you that you have my back regardless. "Why?" Because you deserve it, you are worth it, you deserve the universe. "What's the point?" Your stupid favorite question has no point in itself. But I will still answer it because it's me writing the letter. But you do always have something you gain, something you cherish. But khair :P
